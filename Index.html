About Medicine Expiry Date Checker

This free Medicine Expiry Date Checker helps patients, students, and pharmacists quickly determine if a medicine is still safe to use. Simply enter the expiry date printed on the package and get instant results.

How to Read Medicine Expiry Date?

Most medicines show expiry as MM/YYYY (e.g., 06/2026). A medicine is valid through the last day of the expiry month. So "06/2026" means valid until June 30, 2026.

What is Shelf Life?

Shelf life is the period during which a medicine remains safe and effective when stored properly. In India, most tablet and capsule formulations have a shelf life of 24–36 months from manufacturing date.

Important for D.Pharmacy Students

Expiry date and shelf life calculations are part of Pharmaceutics syllabus and are commonly asked in D.Pharmacy practicals, viva, and Government Pharmacist competitive exams.
